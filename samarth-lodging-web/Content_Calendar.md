## Engagement Strategy

**Post Engagement:**
- Respond to all comments within 2 hours
- Ask questions to encourage discussion
- Use polls and quizzes for interaction
- Share user-generated content

**Community Building:**
- Follow relevant local businesses
- Engage with local tourism accounts
- Participate in local hashtags
- Collaborate with nearby attractions

**Content Optimization:**
- Post during peak hours (10 AM, 7 PM)
- Use high-quality images
- Include clear call-to-action
- Optimize for each platform

## Performance Metrics

**Key Metrics to Track:**
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Reach and impressions
- Click-through rate to website
- Follower growth
- Booking conversions from social media

**Monthly Goals:**
- Engagement rate: 5%+
- Reach: 1,000+ per week
- Website clicks: 50+ per week
- Follower growth: 10% per month

## Content Calendar Notes

**Content Pillars:**
1. **Room Features** - Showcase different room types and amenities
2. **Local Attractions** - Highlight nearby places to visit
3. **Booking Process** - Explain how to book and special offers
4. **Guest Experience** - Share testimonials and guest stories
5. **Behind the Scenes** - Show daily operations and team

**Seasonal Content:**
- **Summer:** Focus on AC rooms and cool destinations
- **Monsoon:** Highlight covered attractions and cozy stays
- **Winter:** Promote warm rooms and winter packages
- **Festivals:** Special offers during local festivals
